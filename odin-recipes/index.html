Pasta Recipes for Spring

Stick-to-your-ribs pasta recipes seem like fall and winter meals, right? Sure, pasta salad knows no season.
But come spring, we love main-dish recipes that amp up the flavors of hearty greens like spinach, broccoli, and asparagus — and fresh produce in general — while letting actual pasta take a bit of a back seat.
These recipes do just that. They may even put a bit of, ahem, spring in your step.
